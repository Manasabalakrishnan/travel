7-Day Trip Itinerary from Bhimavaram to Hyderabad

Day 1: Departure from Bhimavaram to Hyderabad

Morning: Depart early in the morning from Bhimavaram. The journey by road will take around 6-7 hours.

Afternoon: Arrive in Hyderabad and check into your hotel. Rest and refresh.

Evening: Visit Necklace Road and Lumbini Park. Enjoy a boat ride in Hussain Sagar Lake.

Day 2: Historical and Cultural Exploration

Morning: Visit Charminar, explore Laad Bazaar for traditional jewelry and bangles.

Afternoon: Visit Golconda Fort and Qutb Shahi Tombs.

Evening: Head to Salar Jung Museum to explore the extensive collection of art and antiques.

Day 3: Modern Hyderabad and Shopping

Morning: Visit Ramoji Film City for a studio tour and fun activities.

Afternoon: Enjoy a peaceful visit to NTR Gardens and Hussain Sagar Lake then,
go to Bidri restaurent which is of 1.6 km from Hussain sagar and enjoy the Hyderabadi Biryani .

Evening: Shop at Banjara Hills or Jubilee Hills and dine at a local restaurant. .

Day 4: Day Trip to Nagarjuna Sagar

Morning: Travel to Nagarjuna Sagar Dam (approximately 3 hours from Hyderabad).

Afternoon: Take a boat ride to Nagarjunakonda Island, explore the archaeological museum and ruins.

Evening: Return to Hyderabad by evening and enjoy some hyderabadi street food

Day 5: Temples and Parks

Morning: Visit Birla Mandir for a panoramic view of the city.

Afternoon: Visit Chilkur Balaji Temple and Shilparamam arts village.

Evening: Enjoy a peaceful evening at the NTR Gardens or the Buddha Statue by the lake.

Day 6: Nature and Wildlife Exploration

Morning: Start the day with a walk through KBR National Park.

Afternoon: Explore the Nehru Zoological Park or head to Sanghi Temple.

Evening: Relax at Necklace Road or explore Pochampally village for traditional weaving.

Day 7: Final Day of Relaxation & Departure

Morning: Spend your last day shopping at Wonderla and have an amazing play time .

Afternoon: Visit any last attractions, like the Nehru Planetarium, or just relax at a café.

Evening: Eat awesome Hyderabadi haleem and Depart for Bhimavaram in the evening (book a train, bus, or drive back).
