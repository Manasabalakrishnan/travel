7-Day Trip Itinerary from Bhimavaram to Hyderabad

Destinations Home

Day 1: Departure from Bhimavaram to Hyderabad

Morning: Depart early in the morning from Bhimavaram. The journey by road will take around 6-7 hours.

Afternoon: Arrive in Hyderabad and check into your hotel. Rest and refresh.

Evening: Visit Necklace Road and Lumbini Park. Enjoy a boat ride in Hussain Sagar Lake.

Day 2: Historical and Cultural Exploration

Morning: Visit Charminar, explore Laad Bazaar for traditional jewelry and bangles.

Afternoon: Visit Golconda Fort and Qutb Shahi Tombs.

Evening: Head to Salar Jung Museum to explore the extensive collection of art and antiques.

Day 3: Modern Hyderabad and Shopping

Morning: Visit Ramoji Film City for a studio tour and fun activities.

Afternoon: Visit NTR Gardens and then enjoy Biryani at Bidri restaurant.

Evening: Shop at Banjara Hills or Jubilee Hills and dine at a local restaurant.

Day 4: Day Trip to Nagarjuna Sagar

Morning: Travel to Nagarjuna Sagar Dam (3 hrs from Hyderabad).

Afternoon: Boat ride to Nagarjunakonda Island, museum visit.

Evening: Return and enjoy street food in Hyderabad.

Day 5: Temples and Parks

Morning: Visit Birla Mandir.

Afternoon: Chilkur Balaji Temple and Shilparamam arts village.

Evening: Relax at NTR Gardens or Buddha Statue.

Day 6: Nature and Wildlife

Morning: KBR National Park walk.

Afternoon: Visit Sanghi Temple or Nehru Zoo Park.

Evening: Pochampally village & Necklace Road.

Day 7: Final Day & Departure

Morning: Wonderla amusement park fun.

Afternoon: Relax at a café or visit Nehru Planetarium.

Evening: Enjoy Haleem and return to Bhimavaram.
