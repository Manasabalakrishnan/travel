3-Day Trip Itinerary to Vizag

Day 1: Beaches & Submarine Museum

Morning: Arrive in Vizag and relax at your hotel. Head to RK Beach for some sun and sand.

Afternoon: Visit INS Kurusura Submarine Museum and explore the naval history.

Evening: Stroll along the beach road, enjoy local snacks, and watch the sunset.

Day 2: Kailasagiri & Rushikonda Beach

Morning: Ride the ropeway to Kailasagiri and enjoy panoramic city views.

Afternoon: Head to Rushikonda Beach for water sports and chill time.

Evening: Dine at a seaside restaurant with a view of the Bay of Bengal.
