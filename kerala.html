7-Day Trip Itinerary from Bhimavaram to Kerala

Day 1: Arrival at Kochi

Morning: Arrive at Cochin and check into your hotel nearby or visit Hotel centre point.

Afternoon: Explore Fort Kochi, Dutch Palace, and Jewish Synagogue.

Evening: Relax at Marine Drive and witness the sunset at Cherai Beach which is 26.1km from it.

Day 2: Munnar Exploration

Morning: Drive to Munnar (approx 4-5 hours) and check-in at the hotel nearby or else you can try green view hotel.

Afternoon: Visit the Tea Museum and enjoy tea plantations' serene vibes.

Evening: Explore Echo Point and Mattupetty Dam for scenic views.

Day 3: Thekkady & Periyar Wildlife

Morning: Drive to Thekkady and check into your resort (Avadale Thekkadey hotel).

Afternoon: Take a boat ride at Periyar Lake and spot elephants and birds.

Evening: Attend a Kalaripayattu martial arts show and local spice market walk.

Day 4: Alleppey Backwaters

Morning: Drive to Alleppey and check into a traditional houseboat.

Afternoon: Cruise through the backwaters, coconut groves, and paddy fields.

Evening: Watch the sunset from the houseboat and enjoy Kerala cuisine onboard and the most foods in kerala prepared using coconut oil.

Day 5: Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

Morning: Visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary for a peaceful birdwatching walk.

Afternoon: Canoe ride through the narrow canals of Kumarakom village.

Evening: Relax at the resort with sunset views over Vembanad Lake.

Day 6: Varkala & Kovalam Beaches

Morning: Drive to Varkala Beach, enjoy its red cliffs and the Papanasam beach vibe.

Afternoon: Head south to Kovalam for beach relaxation and lighthouse visits.

Evening: Enjoy fresh seafood at a beach shack and sunset over the Arabian Sea.

Day 7: Trivandrum & Departure

Morning: Explore the Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Napier Museum in Trivandrum.

Afternoon: Last-minute shopping at MG Road, and enjoy Kerala Sadya meal.

Evening: Depart from Trivandrum International Airport.
