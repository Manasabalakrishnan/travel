7-Day Trip Itinerary from Bhimavaram to Chennai

Day 1: Departure from Bhimavaram to Chennai

Morning: Depart early from Bhimavaram. The road trip to Chennai takes about 8-9 hours.

Afternoon: Check into your hotel in Chennai and rest.

Evening: Visit Marina Beach and enjoy a peaceful walk by the sea.

Day 2: Historical and Cultural Sites

Morning: Visit the famous Kapaleeshwarar Temple.

Afternoon: Explore Fort St. George and its museum.

Evening: Visit the Government Museum and Art Gallery.

Day 3: Beach Day & Relaxation

Morning: Visit Elliot's Beach and relax in the serene surroundings.

Afternoon: Enjoy a delicious South Indian lunch at a local restaurant.

Evening: Visit the Theosophical Society and the beautiful Adyar Banyan Tree.

Day 4: Day Trip to Kanchipuram

Morning: Take a day trip to Kanchipuram, known for its temples and silk sarees (2-hour drive).

Afternoon: Visit the Kanchi Kamakshi Temple and Ekambareswarar Temple.

Evening: Return to Chennai and relax at a local café.

Day 5: Shopping & Dining

Morning: Explore T. Nagar for shopping, known for its jewelry and clothing markets.

Afternoon: Visit Chennai's popular malls and shop for souvenirs.

Evening: Dine at a famous South Indian restaurant and enjoy Chettinad cuisine.

Day 6: Exploring the City

Morning: Visit the famous San Thome Basilica.

Afternoon: Explore the pristine beaches of Kovalam.

Evening: Visit the beautiful Valluvar Kottam memorial.

Day 7: Final Day & Departure

Morning: Visit the Guindy National Park for a nature walk.

Afternoon: Relax and have lunch at a café.

Evening: Depart for Bhimavaram, concluding your trip.
