7-Day Trip Itinerary to Chennai

Day 1: Arrival & Marina Beach

Morning: Arrive in Chennai, check into your hotel or else stay in Blue city palace.

Afternoon: Explore Marina Beach — the world's second longest beach and also visit Connemara Library-A Grand Structure .

Evening: Walk along the shore, enjoy street snacks like Sundal and Bhaji.

Day 2: Mylapore Temples & Santhome Basilica

Morning: Start your day with eating chennai special sambharidli then Visit Kapaleeshwarar Temple — a stunning example of Dravidian architecture.

Afternoon: Walk to Santhome Cathedral Basilica — built over Saint Thomas's tomb.

Evening: Relax at Elliot’s Beach and sample fresh seafood nearby.

Day 3: Fort St. George & Museums

Morning: Tour Fort St. George — the British East India Company's first fortress in India.

Afternoon: Explore the Government Museum — art, archeology, and history galore.

Evening: Take a walk at Semmozhi Poonga Botanical Gardens.

Day 4: Arts & Shopping

Morning: Visit Kalakshetra Foundation for traditional dance and craft culture.

Afternoon: Stroll around Cholamandal Artists’ Village for art exhibits.

Evening: Shop at T Nagar for sarees and jewelry galore!

Day 5: Mahabalipuram Day Trip

Morning: Drive to Mahabalipuram (approx. 1.5 hours).

Afternoon: Explore Shore Temple, Arjuna's Penance, and Pancha Rathas.

Evening: Return to Chennai for dinner at Mahabs Fisherman’s Cove.

Day 6: Modern Chennai & Street Food

Morning: Visit Phoenix MarketCity for modern shopping and entertainment.

Afternoon: Explore Guindy National Park for wildlife and nature trails.

Evening: Try local Tamil-style seafood at Besant Nagar Beach eateries.

Day 7: Departure

Morning: Breakfast at Amethyst Café — garden-style luxury dining.

Afternoon: Last-minute shopping at Express Avenue Mall.

Evening: Eat famous chennai thali and departure from Chennai with lovely memories!
